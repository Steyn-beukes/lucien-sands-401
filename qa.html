Frequently Asked Questions

What time is check-in and check-out at Lucien Sands 401?
Check-in is from 2:00 PM and check-out is before 10:00 AM. We kindly ask guests to respect these times for smooth transitions.
Is there secure parking available at the property?
Yes, there is one secure garage parking space available inside the complex with a remote-controlled gate for your peace of mind.

Is the kitchen fully equipped for cooking meals?
Absolutely. The kitchen includes a stove, oven, microwave, fridge, kettle, toaster, cutlery, crockery and stainless steel cooking utensils.
Are towels, linen and toilet paper provided?
Yes. The apartment includes fresh linen, bath towels, and a starter roll of toilet paper per bathroom. Please bring your own beach towels.
Can I bring my pets to the apartment?
Unfortunately, pets are not allowed in the apartment or on the complex premises.
Is the apartment serviced daily during our stay?
Daily servicing is not included, but cleaning can be arranged during your stay for an additional fee. Let us know in advance!
Is there Wi-Fi and a smart TV available in the apartment?
Yes! The unit is equipped with fast uncapped Wi-Fi and a smart TV with access to streaming apps. Just sign in with your own account.
Is the apartment wheelchair accessible or suitable for guests with limited mobility?
Yes, the apartment is accessible via a lift in the building, making it convenient for those who prefer to avoid stairs.
Can we use the complex swimming pool freely during our stay?
Yes, the pool is available to all guests at any time.
